Gorillas

Wildlife Wonders Park invites you to discover the magnificent world of gorillas. These awe-inspiring great apes, our closest living relatives, are known for their incredible strength, intelligence, and deep family bonds. Gorillas live in close-knit groups led by a dominant silverback male, and their expressive faces and gentle nature captivate visitors. Gorillas are primarily herbivores, and their vegetarian diet consists of leaves, stems, and fruits, making them essential for forest ecosystems. Tragically, gorilla populations are under threat due to habitat destruction, poaching, and diseases. Our commitment is to raise awareness about these remarkable creatures and the critical need for their conservation. Join us in celebrating the wonder of gorillas, learn about their vital role in the wild, and support our efforts to ensure their survival for generations to come.
